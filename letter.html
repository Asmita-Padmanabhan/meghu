for you, always

Last year, this day, I wrote you a letter about how everyone needs to find their Meghu, because everyone deserves to find a person who is a replica of themselves to truly see who they are. One year later, I stand by that assertion, but for very different reasons. This year has made me discover how different we are, and yet how similar!

It still surprises me that a woman like you would love me and so deeply too - but putting that aside, thank you for everything. It's not easy to put up with me, and yet you do! I can never thank you enough for all the love, encouragement, and care. It warms my heart to a point where I don’t even know how to express the happiness and love I feel.

You keep reminding me that I look pretty and even pointed out that I’ve “glowed up” over the past year. Well, beauty just shows up when someone you love finds you beautiful. All of it is just your presence, your love, and your light. I am not even kidding here, the assurance you give me!

I have a lot to learn from you. Your patience, your empathy, and your morals that make you the warm, mature woman that you are. I strive to do so, little by little. I will always look up to you in awe.

Thank you, Azhagi, for marrying me, for being the one with whom I excitedly share even the littlest of things, for being the biggest cheerleader to Musical Musings, for expressing your love, teaching me to do so, and for being my partner in this life :)

With loads of love,
Achmi
